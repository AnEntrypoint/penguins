🐧 Adelie Penguin

Pygoscelis adeliae

Least Concern
← Back to all species
Scientific NamePygoscelis adeliae
Height46-71 cm
Weight3.8-8.2 kg
Population10 million
RangeEntire Antarctic continent and nearby islands
HabitatAntarctic coastline, pack ice, rocky islands
DietAntarctic krill, fish, squid
Breeding SeasonOctober to February
Clutch Size2 eggs
Lifespan10-20 years

⚠ Threats

  • Sea ice changes from climate warming
  • Krill competition with fisheries
  • Increased snowfall disrupting nesting
Fun Fact:

Adelie penguins migrate up to 17,600 km round-trip each year, one of the longest migrations of any bird.

Data sourced from IUCN Red List, BirdLife International, and Antarctic Treaty resources.
