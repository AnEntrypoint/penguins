🐧 Erect-crested Penguin

Eudyptes sclateri

Endangered

Scientific NameEudyptes sclateri
Height50-70 cm
Weight3.2-5.3 kg
Population150,000
RangeBounty Islands and Antipodes Islands, New Zealand
HabitatRocky shores and tussock grassland on remote sub-Antarctic islands
DietKrill, squid, small fish
Breeding SeasonSeptember to February
Clutch Size2 eggs
Lifespan15-20 years

⚠ Threats

  • Population has declined over 70% since the 1970s for poorly understood reasons
  • Ocean productivity changes
  • Fisheries competition
Fun Fact:

Erect-crested penguins can raise and lower their yellow crest feathers voluntarily, which no other crested penguin species can do.
