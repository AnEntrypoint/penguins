⚠ Threats

  • Stoat and weasel predation on chicks and eggs
  • Habitat disturbance from logging
  • Dog attacks in coastal areas
Fun Fact:

Fiordland penguins are exceptionally secretive breeders, nesting deep in dense rainforest and rarely seen by humans during the breeding season.

Facts about Fiordland Penguin

species

The Fiordland penguin, also known as the Tawaki in Māori, breeds in the rainforests and rocky shores of New Zealand's Fiordland region. It is one of the most elusive penguin species, rarely seen at sea.
