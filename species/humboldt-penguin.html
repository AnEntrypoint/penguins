⚠ Threats

  • El Niño events reducing prey
  • Overfishing
  • Guano harvesting destroying nest sites
Fun Fact:

Humboldt penguins nest in burrows excavated from guano deposits, and historic guano harvesting for fertilizer devastated many colonies.

Facts about Humboldt Penguin

habitat

The Humboldt penguin inhabits the western coast of South America, from Peru to Chile, in areas influenced by the cold, nutrient-rich Humboldt Current. It nests in burrows and caves along rocky coastlines.
