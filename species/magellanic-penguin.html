Clutch Size2 eggs
Lifespan25-30 years

⚠ Threats

  • Oil pollution during offshore migration
  • Overfishing of prey species
  • Climate change shifting prey distribution northward
Fun Fact:

Magellanic penguins are highly faithful to their mates and nesting burrows, returning to the same site year after year.

Facts about Magellanic Penguin

habitat

Magellanic penguins breed in coastal Patagonia in Argentina and Chile, as well as on the Falkland Islands. They migrate north to warmer waters during the austral winter, sometimes traveling thousands of kilometers.
