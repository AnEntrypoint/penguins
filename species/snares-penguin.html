Population63,000
RangeSnares Islands, New Zealand
HabitatDense Olearia forest and rocky shores on predator-free island
DietKrill, squid, small fish
Breeding SeasonSeptember to January
Clutch Size2 eggs
Lifespan15-20 years

⚠ Threats

  • Limited range making entire population vulnerable to single events
  • Ocean warming affecting prey
  • Potential introduction of mammalian predators
Fun Fact:

Snares penguins breed exclusively on The Snares archipelago, one of the smallest and most restricted ranges of any penguin species.
